BetterCompass

What is BetterCompass ?

BetterCompass is a test that will class your political views on a two-dimensional octagonal graph. It both shows your economic leanings and your civic positions.

Why use this version ?

BetterCompass does not mix social or cultural ideas with the economic and civic axis, as opposed to other compass tests. Your results will then be representative of only those two axes.
